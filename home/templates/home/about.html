About Wavaré

Wavaré is an app created by Brandon Pirrocco that gives you realtime forecasts for the biggest waves at some of Portugal's most famous beaches. Wavaré is inspired by Brandon's Portuguese heritage and love for the beauty of the Silver Coast. The app even draws the inspiration for its name from the home of the biggest waves in the world, Nazaré. Brandon's mother used to visit the once quaint fishing village when she was a child growing up in Portugal, and he strives to bring a piece of that world to Wavaré's users.
